Program Overview

Welcome to the University of South Carolina's Graduate School. Here you will find all the information you need about our graduate programs, application process, and more.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Official transcripts
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose
  • GRE scores (if applicable)

How to Apply

To apply, please follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit all required documents.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Check the status of your application online.
