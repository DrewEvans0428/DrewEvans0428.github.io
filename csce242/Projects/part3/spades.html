Spades

Spades is a pulse-pounding, cooperative card game, where players bet on and try to win individual rounds, or “tricks.” It’s best played with 4 people (but can be played with only 2), so gather a few friends and grab a standard deck of 52 cards. We’ll show you how to deal the cards, play the game, and tally scores to find the final winner, so get that deck good and shuffled!
